Welcome to the RPG game "Dragon Repeller"

Introduction:

I made this game by following a project tutorial from a course 
on freeCodeCamp in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. 

This was a the first thing I had to do in JavaScript
so it went pretty fast. But this way of learning while building
a project is a great way to learn. You learn from doing something
which helps you get a better understanding of what you are
learning. And it is a ton of fun making a game at the same time. 

Game Manual:

In this game, you are a lone fighter who needs to help a village
from monsters who have taken over the village. You can buy health
with gold which is won by slaying the monsters and trading your
weapons. Each monster has a higher HP and can return more gold 
than the previous one. But be careful, you may miss an attack 
which could prevent you from beating the game. Good luck!
